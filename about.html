ABOUT ME

My name is Bizuayehu Debre, I was born and grew up in Metu, Ilu Ababor, Ethiopia. I attended my elementary and high school In Metu town. I attended my college degree at Addis Ababa University in literature and graduated in 2010.I started working for a construction company as an office assistant and translator for two years after my graduation. I had a deep interest in technology and decided to pursue another degree and joined New Generation University College and got my Computer Science Degree in 2016, the same year I got married.
I have worked for few months after graduation and then decided to work on my own business. I just got my second child and I thank God for all his blessing. I like helping people. My interest is to reach to disadvantaged people who did not have access to education in my hometown and if I can throughout the world.
